Fun Projects!

Objective:

To create fun, unique work that reflects my personality.

Solution:

Made a geometric version of my dog, that can now be printed as a coloring page.

A bit about the project.

I am an absolute animal lover, but my favourite animal on the planet is my dog. He's the most unique, smart, and interesting dog I have ever met. I needed a way to show my unique side, while doing something I love - hanging out with my dog. I took some time to photograph him and represent him for what he is: unique.
