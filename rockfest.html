Rockfest

Objective:

The objective was to promote my favorite festival of the summer in Montebello.

Solution:

I came up with a quick stop-motion video with a typographic collage of all of the bands attending the festival.

What is Rockfest?

Rockfest is a very popular Canadian festival where people from all over the world join together to getaway for a weekend. It is a rock, punk and metal festival filled with over 150 bands and performers. The festival is not just about the music, but the experience, atmosphere and everyone coming together for a weekend.


Photo to the right is a shot of the main stage mid-day waiting for Sum 41!

I created the collage, printed the collage on large, thick card stock, and cut every image out of it! From there I started my stop-motion. I got a helper to take photographs of every single movement of the images. Over 300 images later I was able to finalize the project and output!
